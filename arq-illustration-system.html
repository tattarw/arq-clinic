the arq. illustration system

A super-minimal visual language built from arq's own DNA — the dot. Every illustration is constructed from single-weight lines and dots in each card's accent color. The dots reference the signature "arq." period. Lines connect them into meaning. Nothing more.

The Rules

1px stroke weight, always. Dots at 3–5px radius at key intersection points. No fills except dots. No gradients. Dashed lines for secondary/atmospheric elements. Each illustration uses its card's accent color at 2–3 opacity levels.

Why It's arq

The period in "arq." isn't decoration — it's a statement of precision. These illustrations extend that philosophy. Every dot is placed with intention. Every line serves a purpose. It's clinical precision rendered as art.

The Feeling

Aesop meets medical diagram. Elegant enough for a luxury brand, precise enough for a physician's office. The restraint is the point — it signals that arq trusts its audience to appreciate subtlety.
