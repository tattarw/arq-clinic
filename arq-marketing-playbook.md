## 1. BRAND VOICE GUIDE

### Core Tone Characteristics
- **Confident**: We know what we're talking about. No hedging, no marketing fluff.
- **Scientific**: Every claim is backed by peer-reviewed research. We cite studies, not feelings.
- **Direct**: Cut to the signal. Respect reader time. No unnecessary words.
- **Never Salesy**: We're not convincing you to buy something you don't need. We're providing tools for those who already know they want optimization.

### Comparable Brands
- The Minimalist (clothing): Radical simplicity, rebellious against overconsumption
- Huberman (communication): Deep science, practical application, zero marketing tricks
- Precision Nutrition: Educational authority, evidence-based, professional tone

### Voice in Practice

**AVOID:**
- "Unlock your potential" / "Transform your life" (generic self-help)
- Exclamation marks (except rare emphasis)
- Superlatives without evidence ("BEST modafinil in India")
- Testimonials styled as hype ("My life changed!!!!")
- Social proof numbers without context
- Vague wellness language

**USE:**
- Specific mechanisms: "BPC-157 increases GDNF and HGF production, potentially improving tissue repair"
- Measurement language: "Most users report onset within 45-60 minutes"
- Honest limitations: "Works for ~70% of the population. Genetic variants in CYP3A4 may reduce efficacy"
- Research citations: "Per Ashcroft et al. (2012), modafinil inhibits dopamine reuptake..."
- Action-oriented: "Take this before 10am. Half-life is ~15 hours."

### Key Messages
1. **We source from verified manufacturers.** Every supplier is vetted. Purity tested. Cold chain maintained.
2. **Prescription-backed.** Licensed doctors vet your health status. This isn't a supplement.
3. **Transparent pricing.** No markup nonsense. You see wholesale cost + our margin clearly.
4. **Biohacking isn't mystical.** It's applied biology. We educate alongside distribution.

## 6. REFERRAL PROGRAM

### Mechanics

**Share Code Method:**
- Each user generates unique referral code (e.g., ARQUSER123)
- Share via WhatsApp, SMS, or direct link
- Referred friend signs up → first order within 30 days → both earn credits

**Incentive Structure:**
- Referrer: ₹200 credit per successful referral
- Referee: ₹200 credit on first order
- Maximum earning: ₹2,000/month per user (10 referrals) to prevent gaming

**How Credit Works:**
- Credits apply at checkout (auto-applied to lowest-priced item first)
- Credit doesn't expire (account-specific)
- Can be used across any product

**Tracking & Validation:**
- Referral link must be used (tracked via UTM parameters)
- Referred user must complete sign-up + doctor consultation
- Order must be placed within 30 days of referral sign-up
- Email confirmation sent when referral is validated

### Promotion Strategy

**Timing:**
- Launch Month 3 after platform stabilization
- Heavy promotion in Week 9-10 (calculated announcement)
- Ongoing evergreen promotion

**Where to Promote:**
- In-app: Prompt after order confirmation + monthly email
- Instagram: Carousel ad series, Stories teasers, captions
- Email: Dedicated referral campaign emails
- WhatsApp: Group messages from doctors to their patients
- Peer-to-peer incentive: Leaderboard (top referrers of month get bonus ₹500)

**Messaging:**
- "We compensate word-of-mouth because trust is everything. Refer a friend, earn ₹200. Both of you do."
- Lead with "we reward referrals" not "we need referrals" (positioning)

### Anti-Fraud Measures
- Require doctor approval after second referral to same doctor
- Flag referrals from same IP address
- Monitor for referral code reselling
- Manual review if user gets 20+ referrals in 30 days
