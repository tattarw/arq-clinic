06 · Paid Search Strategy

When to pay.
When not to.

Most Arq keywords have CPC of $0 — nobody is bidding. That's unusual and temporary. It means organic is the right play now. But a few categories warrant paid spend from day one.
Modafinil / Armodafinil
Don't bid yet
CPC is $0 on all modafinil keywords. Zero advertiser competition. Google likely restricts pharmaceutical advertising on these terms anyway. Organic is the play. Write great content. Rank for free.
Finasteride / Hair loss
Bid in Month 3
"Finasteride 1mg" CPC $0.19. "Minoxidil online" CPC $0.31. "Hairgrow tablet" CPC $0.18. Low CPCs, high commercial intent. Man Matters spends heavily here. Worth a ₹15,000/month test budget in Month 3 once the product pages are live and converting.
BPC-157 / Peptides
Bid now — small budget
"BPC 157 uses" CPC $0.34. "BPC 157 peptide" CPC $0.22. "BPC 157 india" CPC $0.23. Very low CPC. High intent. Nobody else bidding. A ₹5,000/month budget captures every serious searcher. Run this from launch.
Semaglutide / Ozempic
Don't bid
"Ozempic injection" CPC $22.23. Completely uneconomic. Google may restrict GLP-1 ads. Organic content is the play here — "generic semaglutide india," "is semaglutide available in india" are zero-competition organic wins.
Online doctor / Telemedicine
Bid in Month 2
"Online prescription india" CPC $0.20. "Online doctor consultation" CPC $0.36. These are broad but targetable. Small budget, tight geo (Mumbai, Delhi, Bangalore). ₹10,000/month test.
Paid strategy summary
Month 1: ₹5,000/month on BPC-157 keywords only. Month 2: Add ₹10,000 on telemedicine/online prescription. Month 3: Add ₹15,000 on finasteride/hair loss. Total launch paid budget: ₹30,000/month maximum. Organic is the real play.
