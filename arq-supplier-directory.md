## SECTION 8: PROCUREMENT WORKFLOW & TIMELINE

### Typical Procurement Cycle

**T+0 (Day 0):** Low inventory alert triggered
- Inventory hits reorder point
- Ops manager initiates procurement order

**T+1 (Day 1):** Supplier order placed
- PO emailed to supplier with delivery address
- Payment arrangement confirmed (50% advance, 50% on delivery OR net 30)
- Delivery address confirmed

**T+5 to T+10 (Days 5-10):** Goods in transit
- Tracking provided by supplier/courier
- For peptides: Monitor temperature data if tracking available

**T+10 to T+15 (Days 10-15):** Delivery & receipt
- Goods arrive at arq warehouse
- QA inspection (see Section 7 of Operations SOP)
- Payment finalized if on delivery terms

**T+15 (Day 15):** Stock available
- Goods logged into inventory system
- Reserve stock updated

### Supplier Contact Maintenance
- **Quarterly:** Review supplier performance scorecard
- **Monthly:** Order status check-ins
- **Ongoing:** Track new products, pricing updates
