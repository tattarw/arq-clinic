## GENERATION SETTINGS:
- **Aspect ratio:** Landscape, 16:10 (or 1344×840 / 1536×960)
- **Style:** Photorealistic, NOT illustration, NOT 3D render
- **If output looks too AI:** add "editorial photography, practical lighting, Hasselblad medium format, Kodak Portra 400 color science, natural film grain, subtle lens vignette" to the end
- **If skin looks waxy:** add "natural skin texture with pores, not retouched, documentary style"

## WHAT MAKES THE HIMS/RO CARDS WORK:
1. Products FLOAT — they hover above the surface with soft shadows below (not flat-lay)
2. Camera is at EYE LEVEL or slightly above — never directly overhead
3. Backgrounds are RICH gradients, not flat colors — darker edges, brighter center
4. Lighting is from ONE direction — creates dimension, not flat
5. Very few objects — 2-3 items max, not cluttered
6. No text on the products — clean, generic, brandless
7. Lifestyle shots show EMOTION — not posed catalog, but real editorial feeling
8. Both Hims and Hers use a subtle dotted-line graph overlay on lifestyle cards
