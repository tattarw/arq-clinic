# arq. — "One Day, Optimized" Illustration System (v3 — Final)

## PHILOSOPHY FIRST

This is NOT a "draw me 7 pictures" brief. This is a visual system for a premium Indian health optimization brand. The illustrations must feel like they were commissioned from a Tokyo or Copenhagen studio — quiet, considered, expensive. If someone sees these and thinks "stock illustration," we've failed.

**The governing principle: what you DON'T draw matters more than what you do.** Every line must earn its place. If removing a line doesn't destroy meaning, remove it.

---

## REFERENCE ARTISTS — STUDY THESE BEFORE DRAWING

1. **Noritake** (Japanese illustrator) — The primary reference. Study his figure work: 3-8 strokes per human, dot eyes, mitten hands, no facial features beyond dots. His characters feel alive despite extreme reduction. The emotional content comes from POSTURE, not faces.

2. **Geoff McFetridge** — Flat color fields, muted palette, graphic simplicity. His figures are geometric and slightly surreal. Proportions are elongated. Zero texture or gradient.

3. **Jean Jullien** — Bold single-weight outlines, humor through simplicity, every figure is instantly readable at thumbnail size.

4. **Headspace illustration system** — Rounded primitive forms (circles, ovals), warm neutral palette, characters built from simple geometric shapes. Two dots for eyes. No sharp angles anywhere.

**The target sits between Noritake and Headspace — Noritake's line economy with Headspace's warmth.**

---

## TECHNICAL SPECIFICATION

**Line:**
- Single uniform stroke weight: 2px
- Ink color: warm dark brown (#2c2824) — NOT pure black
- Every stroke is a clean, confident vector bezier — no wobble, no roughness, no doubled lines
- Rounded line caps and joins (never squared)
- NO variation in stroke thickness. Period.

**Color:**
- Lines: #2c2824 (warm brown)
- ONE fill accent: #b8922e (warm gold)
- The gold appears as a FLAT FILL on exactly one small object per scene
- No gradients. No shadows. No texture. No opacity variations.
- Everything else is unfilled outline only

**Geometry:**
- All corners rounded — no sharp angles on any form
- Figures built from ovals and curves, not straight lines
- Hair = one single closed shape
- Hands = rounded mitten shape, or a simple curve. NEVER individual fingers.
- Feet/shoes = one simple shape each
- Furniture = 2-3 lines maximum (a chair is an L-shape and a back line. A table is a horizontal and two verticals. A bed is two horizontal lines.)

**Face:**
- Two dots for eyes. NOTHING ELSE.
- No nose. No mouth. No eyebrows. No ear detail.
- Expression comes from: head tilt, shoulder position, posture
- The two dots should be small and placed with intent — their spacing and position on the head shape conveys everything

**Body:**
- Head: oval
- Torso: one closed shape (t-shirt indicated by sleeve curve only — no collar, no seams, no hem line)
- Arms: single lines ending in mitten hands
- Legs: two lines, slightly tapered
- Total strokes for a standing figure: 8-12 maximum

**Canvas:**
- 7 separate images
- Each: 800px wide × 400px tall
- Transparent background — absolutely no background rectangle or fill
- Character should occupy roughly 40-50% of the frame
- Remaining space is EMPTY — this negative space is the luxury

## THE 7 SCENES

Each scene title is for internal reference only — do NOT include text labels in the output.

### 1. MORNING STRETCH
Standing figure, one arm reaching overhead in a gentle arc. Weight on one leg. Eyes closed (two small downward curves instead of dots). A small mug shape beside him on a surface (one horizontal line).
**Gold fill: the mug.**
Target strokes: ~12

### 2. THE PROTOCOL
Seated figure (chair = 2 lines). One arm extended forward, hand holding a small circle (capsule/pill) between mitten-thumb and mitten-hand. Other arm resting on a table surface (one horizontal line). A glass shape on the table (two lines).
**Gold fill: the capsule.**
Target strokes: ~16

### 3. DEEP WORK
Figure seated at a desk (one horizontal line), leaning slightly forward. A laptop in front of him (one angled rectangle for screen + one horizontal for base). One mitten-hand on the laptop area. Profile view, facing left.
**Gold fill: the laptop screen rectangle.**
Target strokes: ~14

### 4. THE CONSULTATION
Two figures standing, facing each other with a comfortable gap between them. Left figure = our character. Right figure = doctor, indicated ONLY by a slightly longer torso shape (suggesting a coat) — no stethoscope, no cross, no medical props. One figure has a hand slightly raised. The doctor holds a small rectangle (clipboard).
**Gold fill: the clipboard.**
Target strokes: ~20

### 5. THE RUN
Figure in full running stride, profile view facing right. Dynamic forward lean. One leg extended behind, one bent forward. Arms in running position. Hair shape slightly displaced. The most energetic pose — convey speed through the lean angle, not through motion lines or speed marks.
**Gold fill: the sole of the front shoe.**
Target strokes: ~10

### 6. WINDING DOWN
Figure reclined in a chair (3 lines max for chair). One leg crossed over the other. Holding a small rectangle (book/phone) loosely in one hand. Head tilted slightly back. Shoulders visibly dropped/relaxed — this posture should feel like an exhale.
**Gold fill: the book/phone.**
Target strokes: ~15

### 7. SLEEP
Figure lying on side, on a minimal bed (2 horizontal lines). Legs slightly bent. One arm tucked under. Eyes as two small downward curves (closed). A tiny crescent moon shape floating above — the only object in the scene besides the bed. This should be the SIMPLEST scene of all — maximum negative space, minimum lines.
**Gold fill: the crescent moon.**
Target strokes: ~8

## CRITICAL: WHAT NOT TO DO

These are hard failures. Any of these ruins the system:

- ❌ Individual fingers on hands
- ❌ Facial features beyond two dots (no nose, mouth, eyebrows, ears)
- ❌ Interior clothing detail (no collars, seams, buttons, pockets, hems, wrinkles)
- ❌ Detailed furniture (no chair legs with crossbars, no desk drawers, no bed frames with headboard detail)
- ❌ Hair strands — hair is ONE shape
- ❌ Ground shadow or any shadow
- ❌ Motion lines, speed marks, or sweat drops
- ❌ Background elements (no windows, walls, floor textures, plants, clocks on walls)
- ❌ More than one fill color
- ❌ Gradient or texture of any kind
- ❌ Line weight variation
- ❌ Sketchy or rough line quality
- ❌ Cute or playful expressions
- ❌ Any text or label in the illustration
