# arq. — Narrative Illustration Concept

## The Idea
A single character (young Indian man, simple line art) and his dog journey down the page. The page layout IS their world — bento cards are platforms, section dividers are hills, the footer is the horizon. It's playful, warm, and tells a story of someone taking charge of their health.

## Scene Map (top → bottom)

### Scene 1 — Hero area (above bento grid)
**Boy + dog walking confidently to the right**, like they're about to step onto the bento grid.
- Position: Bottom-right of the hero text area, just above the bento grid
- The dog is slightly ahead, tail up
- Boy is relaxed, hands in pockets or one arm swinging
- Feeling: "beginning of a journey"

### Scene 2 — Bento grid, perched on the Hair card
**Boy sitting on the top edge of the Hair Health card**, legs dangling over the side, reading a book/phone.
- Dog sitting next to him, looking down at the grid below
- Position: Sits ON TOP of the card border, breaking the container — half in, half out
- Feeling: "doing research, being intentional about health"

### Scene 3 — Between bento grid and How It Works
**Boy + dog walking across**, maybe the dog is running ahead and the boy is jogging to catch up
- Position: In the gap between sections, overlapping both slightly
- Feeling: "momentum, moving forward"

### Scene 4 — Spotlight section (Hair protocol)
**Boy looking in a mirror** (simple oval shape), touching his hair confidently
- Dog waiting patiently nearby
- Position: Right side of the spotlight, atmospheric
- Feeling: "confidence, self-care"

### Scene 5 — Stats bar area
**Dog doing a little victory jump/spin** (just the dog, no boy)
- Position: Small, tucked between stats
- Feeling: "celebration, lightness"

### Scene 6 — Weight spotlight section
**Boy running/jogging with dog**, both in motion, energetic
- Position: Left side of spotlight
- Feeling: "vitality, action"

### Scene 7 — Doctors section
**Boy shaking hands with a figure** (representing a doctor), dog sitting politely
- Position: Subtle, near the doctor grid
- Feeling: "trust, partnership"

### Scene 8 — Footer area
**Boy and dog sitting together looking at a sunset/horizon**, seen from behind
- Position: Above or within the footer
- Feeling: "peace, the long game, longevity"

## Style Rules
- **Line weight**: 1.5-2px consistent stroke
- **Color**: Single accent per scene — use arq gold (#b8922e) as the primary fill accent
- **Background**: Transparent (no cream fill — these float ON the page)
- **Canvas**: Each scene on its own transparent canvas, max 800×400px
- **Character**: Simple, not detailed face — more Noritake/Geoff McFetridge than realistic
- **Dog**: Small-medium breed, floppy ears, expressive tail — think golden retriever puppy energy
- **No text labels** in any illustration
- **Consistent character** across all scenes — same proportions, same style

## Technical Notes
- Each scene is a separate SVG file
- They're positioned with CSS absolute/fixed positioning
- Some scenes use parallax (slight scroll offset) for depth
- The character breaks containers — sits ON card edges, walks ACROSS section borders
- On mobile: show only scenes 1, 2, 6, 8 (reduce to key moments)
