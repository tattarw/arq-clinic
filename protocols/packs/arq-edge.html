Absolute contraindication
DO NOT combine sildenafil with nitrates (nitroglycerin, isosorbide). This can cause severe, potentially fatal hypotension. If you develop chest pain, stop immediately and seek emergency care.
Interactions

Drug interactions

Both modafinil and sildenafil are CYP3A4 substrates. Modafinil may slightly decrease sildenafil bioavailability, but clinically insignificant. The key concern is with external medications.

!
Nitrates (any form)
Absolute contraindication with sildenafil. Can cause life-threatening hypotension. Do not use together under any circumstance.
Mechanism: Additive vasodilation → severe hypotension
!
Modafinil + Sildenafil CYP3A4 substrate interaction
Modafinil may induce CYP3A4 over 2–4 days, slightly reducing sildenafil levels. Unlikely to be clinically significant, but monitor response. As-needed dosing minimizes risk.
Mechanism: CYP3A4 induction → decreased sildenafil AUC
i
Ashwagandha + thyroid medications
Ashwagandha may enhance thyroid function and TSH suppression. If on levothyroxine or other thyroid meds, monitor TSH every 6–8 weeks. Usually not an issue at 600mg doses.
Mechanism: Enhanced thyroid hormone conversion
i
Blood pressure and heart rate monitoring
If on antihypertensive meds, the combination of modafinil + sildenafil may lower BP. Baseline cardiovascular fitness and medical screening essential. Have BP checked regularly.
Mechanism: Modafinil sympathomimetic, sildenafil vasodilatory
