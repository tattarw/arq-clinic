Interactions

Drug interactions

Modafinil is the primary driver of drug interactions in this stack. L-Theanine, Alpha-GPC, and Magnesium Glycinate have negligible hepatic metabolism.

!
Oral contraceptives (ethinyl estradiol)
Modafinil induces CYP3A4, reducing OCP efficacy by ~18%. Use backup contraception or alternative during protocol and 1 month after.
Mechanism: CYP3A4 induction → increased estrogen clearance
i
L-Theanine + antihypertensives
L-Theanine may mildly potentiate blood pressure-lowering effects. Monitor BP if on ACE inhibitors or calcium channel blockers. Unlikely to cause clinical issues at 200mg doses.
Mechanism: Additive vasodilatory effects
i
Alpha-GPC + anticholinesterase inhibitors
Alpha-GPC boosts acetylcholine availability. If on donepezil or other cholinesterase inhibitors (dementia treatment), monitor for cholinergic side effects. Not an issue in healthy users.
Mechanism: Additive cholinergic enhancement
+
Caffeine
Synergistic wakefulness promotion. Safe to combine but reduce caffeine by 50% (e.g., 100mg coffee instead of 200mg). Higher doses increase jitter and anxiety risk despite L-Theanine buffering.
Mechanism: Additive adenosine antagonism + dopaminergic activation
