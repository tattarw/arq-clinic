Drug interactions

Molecule synergies

✓
Testosterone + Semaglutide
No direct pharmacokinetic interaction. Complementary recomposition: testosterone builds muscle, semaglutide reduces appetite. Combined effect is additive.
Mechanism: Different receptors (AR vs GLP-1R), non-overlapping pathways
✓
Testosterone + Metformin
Testosterone increases insulin sensitivity; metformin enhances this. Favorable combination for glucose homeostasis. May enhance body recomposition synergistically.
Mechanism: Both improve GLUT4 translocation and mitochondrial function
⚠
Semaglutide + Metformin (absorption)
Semaglutide delays gastric emptying, which may slow metformin absorption slightly. Unlikely to be clinically significant at these doses. Monitor blood glucose; no dose adjustment needed.
Mechanism: Delayed gastric transit reduces oral bioavailability by ~5-10%
✓
L-Carnitine + Semaglutide
Synergistic for fat oxidation. L-carnitine supports mitochondrial beta-oxidation; semaglutide reduces caloric intake. No interference with carnitine transport.
Mechanism: L-carnitine is substrate for CPT1; semaglutide is unrelated GLP-1 agonist
