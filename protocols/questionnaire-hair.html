Finasteride: Blocks DHT, the hormone responsible for male-pattern hair loss. Prevents further loss and promotes regrowth.

Minoxidil: Topical growth stimulant that increases blood flow to hair follicles and prolongs the growth phase.

Biotin: Essential B-vitamin that strengthens hair structure and supports overall hair health.
Month 1–2: Initial shedding may increase — old hairs making way for new growth. This is completely normal.

Month 3–4: New growth begins. You'll notice less hair in the drain and baby hairs emerging.

Month 5–6+: Visible thickening and regrowth. Maintain with ongoing protocol for sustained results.
Common: Mild scalp irritation from minoxidil. Mostly resolves within 2–4 weeks.
